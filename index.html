From intent to impact

Great ideas die without delivery. My craft is turning intent into infrastructure—clarity of strategy, simple tools, and steady execution. I build for real constraints: low connectivity, limited budgets, busy teachers, curious communities. The outcome isn't noise; it's momentum you can measure—in classrooms, in homes, and in livelihoods.

What it looks like when learning arrives

Khula Box in Action

Khula Box brings local search + AI to classrooms and community hubs, aggregating books, videos, Wikipedia, and science tools—all offline. Purpose-built indexing delivers instant responses and distraction-free learning. Teachers contribute and share lessons through ProScola; communities grow their own knowledge base through Rise Village. This is technology as a supply line for curiosity.

Who I am

I'm a builder at heart—whether I'm designing learning systems or restoring a vintage motorcycle. Based in Basel, Switzerland, I split my time between strategic work with organizations and hands-on projects that keep me grounded.

When I'm not in front of a screen, you'll find me in the garage working on a bike, in the workshop building furniture, or in the kitchen experimenting with fermentation. Each project—whether professional or personal—teaches me something about systems, patience, and getting my hands dirty.

I believe the best solutions come from understanding how things work from the ground up, whether that's a carburetor or a learning platform.
